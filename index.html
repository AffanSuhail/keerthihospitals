Welcome To Keerthi Hospitals

KEERTHI HOSPITALS, a distinguished institution in Madurai with a legacy of over 70 years, is renowned for its compassionate care for women and children. Originating from Dr. Karthiyaini Muthu's esteemed Aruna Clinic, Keerthi Hospitals has evolved into a modern healthcare facility equipped with advanced technologies and a specialized fertility center. Our team of skilled physicians and dedicated healthcare professionals is committed to providing the highest quality care, adhering to the highest ethical standards.
